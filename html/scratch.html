Freddie Freeman



Major League First Baseman for the Los Angeles Dodgers

This is a page dedicated to Freddie Freeman



Freddie Freeman has many accomplishments in his career that make him one of the greatest of all time

Here are but a few:

  1. MVPs
    • 2020 National League MVP
    • 2024 World Series MVP
  2. Awards
    • 2019, 2020, and 2021 Silver Slugger Winner
    • 2018 Gold Glove Winner
  3. World Series Wins
    • 2021 World Series Winner with the Atlanta Braves
    • 2024 World Series Winner with the Los Angeles Dodgers
